gitk discription:


the upper left window are the branches, with yellow dot showing the head. 
there’s branch/remote login/commit message.
The dots might go inward (of the interface) showing
 the tree structure of the branch.

the upper right window are the commit authors, with time on the right


the bottom left are the file changes in diff patch format

the bottom right window are the files impacted by the select commit. 

In the middle there’s SHA ID for the commit.
